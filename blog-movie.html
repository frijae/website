Storyline

Requiem for a Dream follows four interconnected characters whose dreams of happiness and success slowly collapse under the grip of addiction. As each person turns to drugs or obsession as a way to escape their disappointments, their lives spiral further out of control. The film shows how hope, denial, and dependence feed into one another, ultimately leading to devastating physical and emotional consequences.

Plot

The movie follows four people whose lives are connected through addiction and personal dreams. As they chase happiness love and success they slowly lose control over their lives. Their choices push them deeper into obsession leading to tragic consequences for each character.

Run

The movie was released in 2000 and gained attention for its intense storytelling and unique visual style. While it was not a major box office hit it became very popular over time through word of mouth and home media. Today it is considered a cult classic and one of the most talked about films about addiction.
